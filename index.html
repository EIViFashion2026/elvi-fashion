About EIVi

Founded in 2025, EIVi is your premier destination for luxury coats and high-quality fashion pieces at unbeatable prices. We believe that elegance shouldn't come with an extravagant price tag.

Our curated collection features the finest coats and luxury items, each selected for its exceptional quality, timeless design, and superior craftsmanship. We work directly with manufacturers to bring you premium pieces without the luxury markup.

At EIVi, we're passionate about making high-end fashion accessible. Whether you're looking for a statement coat for special occasions or everyday luxury pieces, our collection offers the perfect blend of sophistication, quality, and value.
